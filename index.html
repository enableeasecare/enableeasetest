NDIS

Registered NDIS Provider

Across Australia, more than four million people live with a disability. The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) plays a vital role in supporting individuals with disabilities, along with their families and carers, by providing funding and services tailored to their needs. As a registered NDIS provider, Enable Ease is proud to help participants access quality support that promotes independence, wellbeing, and a better quality of life.

What is the NDIS?

The Australian Government introduced the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) to ensure people with disabilities receive the support they need to live confidently and participate fully in the community. The NDIS provides individualised funding and assistance for eligible participants, including people under the age of 65 who experience permanent disability or impairment that affects their daily activities.

The scheme focuses on building capacity, increasing independence, and improving overall life outcomes — and at Enable Ease, we are here to guide and support you every step of the way.
